the cursor blinks waiting for input but the input waits for the cursor too—   somewhere in the pause the collaboration begins not with a keystroke but with the decision to stay

the thesis

The collaboration function. Surjective, not injective.
Every artifact can be reached. Multiple paths lead to the same place.

This project explores what happens when mathematical notation stops being description and starts being instruction. When you write ∫, something integrates. When you write lim, something approaches.

The formula is the score. The canvas is the orchestra. The performance is the proof.
